Biography

Hi, I'm Peixuan, currently a Mphil student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Prof. Mingming Fan. I got my bachelor's degree from Tiangong University and majored in Mechanical engineering and textile, and I got another master's degree from Sun Yat-Sen University and researched swarm robotics in mixed reality environments. Now my research interests mainly focus on HCI, including BCI, VR, and companion robots. I am glad to do cross-disciplinary work, and always curious about new and unknown staff.
